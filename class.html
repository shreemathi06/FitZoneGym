Training classes

Cardio Classes

  • Spinning/Cycling: High-intensity indoor cycling workouts.
  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training): Intense cardio sessions involving bursts of high-intensity exercises followed by short rest periods.
  • Zumba: Dance-based cardio workout combining Latin and international music.
  • Step Aerobics: Cardio workout using a step platform.

Strength Training Classes

  • Body Pump: A barbell workout focusing on muscle endurance and strength.
  • Circuit Training: A mix of cardio and strength exercises done in stations.
  • CrossFit: High-intensity strength and conditioning workout with varied functional movements.
  • Boot Camp: Military-style strength and conditioning workout.

Mind and Body Classes

  • Yoga: Classes focusing on flexibility, balance, strength, and relaxation.
  • Pilates: Core-focused classes enhancing strength, posture, and flexibility.
  • Tai Chi: A low-impact, slow-motion exercise promoting flexibility and mindfulness.

Specialized Classes

  • Kickboxing: Combines martial arts techniques with fast-paced cardio.
  • TRX Suspension Training: Resistance training using body weight and a suspension trainer.
  • Aqua Aerobics: Low-impact cardio and strength exercises performed in water.
  • Functional Training: Focuses on exercises that improve everyday movements and activities.

Recovery and Relaxation Classes

  • Stretching Classes: Guided stretching routines for improved flexibility.
  • Foam Rolling: Self-myofascial release classes using foam rollers.
  • Restorative Yoga: Gentle yoga focusing on relaxation and recovery.
