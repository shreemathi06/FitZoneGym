FACILITIES

24/7 Access or Extended Hours

Variety of Equipment

  • A diverse range of cardio machines (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes).
  • Strength training equipment (dumbbells, barbells, kettlebells, resistance machines).
  • Functional fitness tools (medicine balls, battle ropes, resistance bands).

Safety and Hygiene

  • Regular sanitization and cleaning of equipment and facilities.
  • Trained staff for first aid and emergency response.
  • Availability of hand sanitizers and wipes throughout the gym.

Childcare Services

  • On-site daycare or play area for members with children.
  • Trained staff to supervise children during workouts.
  • Storytelling sessions to keep children entertained and active.

Accessible Location and Parking

  • Convenient location with easy access.
  • Ample parking space or bike racks for members.
  • Accessible for people with disabilities.

Flexible Membership Options

  • Different membership plans (e.g., monthly, yearly, pay-as-you-go).
  • Discounts for students, seniors, or families.
  • Options for free trials or guest passes.

Tech Integration

  • Fitness apps or online portals for booking classes, tracking progress.
  • Wearable tech compatibility or gym-provided trackers.
  • Free Wi-Fi for members

Outdoor Spaces

  • Areas for outdoor training or group activities.
  • Green spaces or rooftop areas for yoga or stretching sessions.
  • Wide entrances, ramps, and elevators for wheelchair access.

Wellness Programs

  • Offer holistic programs, including nutrition counseling, meditation sessions, or wellness workshops.
  • Partnerships with physiotherapists, dietitians, or massage therapists.

Personal Training

  • One-on-one or small group training sessions with certified trainers.
  • Customized workout plans and nutritional guidance.
  • Progress tracking and goal setting.
